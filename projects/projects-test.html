Hybrid Fabrication Machine HM01

Explore my adventures in building a machine capable of additive and subtractive manufacturing. Motivated by a lack of tools with which to build my other designs, this project started during the COVID-19 lockdowns of spring 2020. Follow along as I challenge and/or discover the reasons for various conventions in 3d-printer design.
