Projects Nexus

Welcome to the projects nexus, the central hub for navigating to the various project threads stored on this website. Each thread tells the story of another project I have had a hand in that seemed worthy of mentioning.

Perhaps the most universal impulse that engineers the world over share is the desire to make things better. Accordingly, work on these projects is characterized by the continual relation between this impulse and limitations imposed by finite time, energy, and resources. As a result, while projects may be shelved once achieving their stated goal, it is not proper to consider them completed as more improvements can always be made. By reason of the same limitations, projects might go without progress for a time as priority and resources are shifted to other projects.
