Welcome to my Website!

My dream is to inspire through invention. This website is a sort of project portfolio intended to document the development of my eclectic collection of engineering and design talents. However, I created it to be a public repository in the hopes that other ambitious inventors might somehow benefit from my work, even if only a little. I claim to be neither an field-matter expert, nor a master of engineering arts; simply someone excited to bring novel ideas to life and motivated by the prospect of a future containing untold and countless wonders.

About the Author

Austin Wells is currently a student of mechanical engineering at North Carolina State University, where he also works as a student reactor operator at the NC State PULSTAR nuclear reactor. In addition to his studies, Austin is the current mechanical team lead of Pack Bionics, a compeditive prosthetics design club. His current research projects include procedural design of non-spherical rolling interfaces and parametric design of high-dexterity anthropomorphic manipulators.
